About Dj Angerone

Mike Myers, professionnally known as DJ Angerone, is internationnally acclaimed Progressive House & Trance DJ &musician.

I try to experiment with various music genres in every track I make

Since the beginnig of his career in 2010, he has already 5 EPs on a range of worldwide known labels (Booster Records, DSTRC Recordings etc.) and received a number of music awards.
